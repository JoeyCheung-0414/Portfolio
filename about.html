I am a mixed media artist, working with all types of media, like painting, digital media, sculpture, etc. My work revolves around exploring and experimenting with new media. I often incorporate bright colors, and bold line work, and typically have a cheerful undertone. I am inspired by the fantasy genre and anime.Â 
I was born in Hong Kong and raised in California, surrounded by another passionate artist who inspired me. My dad whom I loved to watch as a kid as he worked on his model kits, inspired me to start doing art as a hobby and now pursuing art as a career. I want to instill a sense of wonder into those who view my work and immerse themselves in the worlds I create in my work.
