The Normal Ending

You say that you are only here for a cup of coffee and maybe a snack. You had no interest in other worlds, you merely think it is some sort of event that the cafe put up for sale. As for the skeleton, you believe it's a well-made puppet and someone is controlling it under the table. After all a lot of people like scary gimmicks.
“Very well, here's the menu,” he said.
You have your drink and leave the bar to go on in your boring regular life.
